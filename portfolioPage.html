Murder Squad

An app that simulates a murder mystery dinner party.

The user selects a story to play and a character to play as throughtout the game. You must use your clues and the stories told by other guests to determine who the killer was before the end of the game!

The app uses jquery and the bootstrap and materialize frameworks for the front end while using MySQL for storing, structuring and relating the data on the back end.

Budget Tracker

The app uses jquery and the materialize framework for the front end and uses MySQL for storing, structuring and relating the data on the back end.
